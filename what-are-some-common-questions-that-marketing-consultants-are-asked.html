What are your rates?

This is a question that all consultants get asked at some point. It’s also a question that can be a bit tricky to answer. A marketing consultant’s rates depend on a number of factors, including their experience, the type of client they’re working with, and the amount of time they’ll be spending on the project.In general, the more experience you have and the higher-end your services are, the higher your rates will be. However, it’s also important to note that rates vary widely across different industries.If you’re just starting out as a consultant, you can use this as an opportunity to charge less. If you have extensive experience and are charging higher rates, you can use this as an opportunity to charge more.

This is another question that all consultants get asked at some point. It’s also a question that can be a bit tricky to answer. What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is marketing to individuals through World wide web electronic devices such as computers, cellphones, and tablets. Digital marketing relies on search engines, social networking sites, email, webpages, and applications to interact with prospects and consumers.

Would my business benefit from digital marketing?

Absolutely. Although several businesses continue to be cautious of digital marketing, ignoring this limits your company’s access to social media that most customers look to first and across all hours of the day.
